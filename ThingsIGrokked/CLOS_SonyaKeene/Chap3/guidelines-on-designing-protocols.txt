GuideLines
----------
1. Restrict the user's access to the internal data structures.
   (using export/import in packages)
2. Provide constructor functions for creating the data structures.
3. Design the protocol to anticipate the needs of the users.
4. Allow the protocol to evolve to meet the reasonable needs of users.
5. Design some protocols to be extensible by the user
